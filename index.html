Swim Etiquette

So many thoughts on this. Always choose a lane that matches your speed. If you're in the fast lane because it's empty, but you're really a medium-speed swimmer, move aside when a fast swimmer comes. If there is just one person in the lane, ask if you can split the lane. When a third person arrives, circle swim counter-clockwise. If you find you are slower than the person behind you when circle swimming, stop at the nearest wall and let them proceed ahead of you.
